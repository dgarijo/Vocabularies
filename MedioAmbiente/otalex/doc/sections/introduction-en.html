1. Introduction back to ToC

According to the specifications in [ ATLAS OTALEX ], the Atlas OTALEX C represents the result of a long series of cross-border projects where the fruitful cooperation prevails and territories defend their common interests. The ontology derived from the atlas includes terms and properties to refer to each of the maps included in it. Known ontologies like GeoLinkedData and geonames are used to link resources in the atlas with other external resources.
