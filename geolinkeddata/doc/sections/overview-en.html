2. GeolinkedData: Overview back to ToC

The GeolinkedData network is composed of 3 ontologies. The first one (http://geo.linkeddata.es/ontology/transportes.owl) describes the hydrographic phenomena of a region. The second one (http://geo.linkeddata.es/ontology/transportes.owl) describes the main routes of a region, along with their main points of intersts (i.e., Airports, etc.). Finally, the last one (http://geo.linkeddata.es/ontology/geopolitica.owl) describes the relationship among the different parts of a territory..
